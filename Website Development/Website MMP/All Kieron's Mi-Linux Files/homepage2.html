Welcome to Survey4All, an online survey tool created for individuals looking to acquire real data from real people. With the help of a wide range of tools on our forward thinking platform, you can easily develop, distribute, and analyse your surveys. 1: Click on create surveys page on the navigation bar

2: Choose a survey title, question name along with question type and submit

4: Select the survey you want to vote on and answer the questions and press submit
